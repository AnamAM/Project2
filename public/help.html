Volunteer

When you volunteer at H.A.S. Clinic, you are a vital part of helping to ensure that everyone has access to life-saving blood when they need it. H.A.S Clinic volunteers are essential to our mission because volunteers make it possible for us to provide and protect the community blood supply, to inspire our community to continue to save lives locally, and to always treat everyone we serve as family.

If you are interested in vounteering at H.A.S. Clinic, please fill out the volunteer application form below.

H.A.S. Clinic Volunteering
The minimum age for volunteering is 16.
We will email you for your availability.

Host a Blood Drive

The local blood supply is a shared community resource that bonds us all. H.A.S Blood Clinic is the only truly local blood services provider. And having a thriving local blood supply ensures that the people you see every day, the people you care about the most—family, friends, neighbors, coworkers—always have access to life-saving blood when they need it.

To join the cause and the family by hosting a blood drive, call us at 1 (800)-HAS-LIFE or shoot us an email. It’s that easy!

Donate Money

Whether you are a blood donor who wants to do more, or you are not eligible to donate blood but want to support our life-saving mission, your financial support will help ensure that quality blood products will always be available for every patient in need. As a non-profit organization, we depend on charitable giving to provide for service improvements that cannot always be fully funded otherwise.

For more information regarding any sort of donation, please contact us through our email.
