H.A.S. Blood Clinic has been drawing blood for the past five years and was founded in a place near and dear to our hearts: Houston, Texas. For the past five years, we have been able to serve the community with saving over countless amounts of precious lives. Our H.A.S Clinic has been growing ever since and we are proud to say that our new locations in Austin and San Antonio, Texas will be opening in 2020. We will continue to save lives and be a helping hand to our community because it is the people who drive us to do what we do.
